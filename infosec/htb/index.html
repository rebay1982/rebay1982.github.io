HackTheBox.eu

HackTheBox.eu is a play ground to test your skills in penetration testing or CTFs solving. I do boxes and challenge in my spare time and see them as interesting puzzles to solve. I will do write-ups for them when they get retired as a form of note taking – it helps me remember techniques I’ve tried or keep tabs on the new things I’ve learned to solution the boxes or challenges. Hopefully they can be of use to other folks. Enjoy!
